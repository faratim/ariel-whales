How Ariel Got Interested In Whales

Hello, my name is Ariel!

I got interested in whales when I got my stuffed whale named Whaley. I got him when I was born. And I've loved whales ever since.

I love whales because they do a lot of cool things and I think they are very insteresting.

I would love to swim with a blue whale, that would be awesome! ...but also kind of dangerous.

To me, whales are one of the coolest animals on earth! And blue whales are the biggest animal that ever lived on earth.

And that's how I got interested in whales!
